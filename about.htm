About Maria-Lola

Hi, I'm the founder of Chicks Who Code, a self taught web developer, designer and SEO expert. I studied art & design at Chelsea College of Art in London as well as Animation and Digital Compositing at University of Westminster and Escape Studios. I began designing websites for myself and soon after started to build and develop websites for other people and businesses. This is where my passion for coding began.

My love for technology and art have led me to gain experience in building e-commerce platforms, wordpress websites, bootstrap sites, bespoke projects for weddings, small and creative businesses. My skills also excel in getting you found on google with SEO (Search Engine Optimization).
