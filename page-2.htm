    Here is how I can work with you:

    Step 1:

    This is the initial stage where you will let me know what you are looking to build (business website, landing page, wedding website, ecommerce platform etc.) You can do so by clicking the contact button below.

    Step 2:

    We will then schedule a 30 min call over skype or in person to go over your project in more detail so I have a better understanding of your needs, expectations and idea.

    Step 3:

    Here we sign an agreement going over the work to be done and you approve it moving forward.
